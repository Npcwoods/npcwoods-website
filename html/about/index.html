The Short Version

Why NPCWoods Exists

I worked in urgent care, telemedicine, and primary care for over a decade before starting NPCWoods in 2025. I kept seeing the same thing: people who needed straightforward care—a UTI, a sinus infection, a dental infection they couldn't get treated—but couldn't get it because the system made it too expensive, too slow, or too complicated.

So I built something different. NPCWoods is text-based telemedicine at a $59 flat fee. No video call. No waiting room. No paperwork. You text me what's going on, I take a thorough history, and if treatment is appropriate, I send a prescription to your pharmacy. That's it.

“This isn't a venture-backed tech startup with chatbots and clinical directories. It's one nurse practitioner who answers every text personally.”

How I Practice

What to Expect

When you text NPCWoods, you're texting me, not a call center, not a chatbot, not an AI. I personally read every message, take a thorough history, and make the clinical decision.

If medication is appropriate, I send a prescription to your pharmacy. If something needs in-person care, I'll tell you straight up, no charge for the honesty. I'd rather lose a $59 visit than put you in a bad spot.

My approach is radically transparent. Real talk, no corporate BS. You deserve to know exactly what's going on with your body and exactly what I'm thinking. That's the kind of care I'd want for my own family.
