Pharmacy Partnership Program

Give your uninsured patients
access to affordable care

Partner with NPCWoods so your patients who can't afford a provider visit can get evaluated and treated, and pick up their prescription right at your counter. Zero cost to your pharmacy.

The Problem

You see it every day

A patient walks up to your counter needing a refill or a new medication, but they don't have a current prescription. They can't afford an urgent care visit. So they either go without treatment or end up in the ER for something simple.

Meanwhile, your pharmacy loses a fill and your patient goes home without the medication they need.

NPCWoods fixes this. We give your patients a way to see a licensed provider by text for $59, and we send the prescription right back to you.

How It Works

Simple for you. Simple for your patients.

1

We give you a QR code for your counter

Your patient scans it with their phone. It takes them to a page where they can text Chris Woods, a board-certified nurse practitioner, about their symptoms.

2

We evaluate and treat your patient

Chris reviews their case via text (no video call, no app download), creates a treatment plan, and determines if medication is needed. The patient pays $59, that's it.

3

The prescription goes to YOUR pharmacy

We send the script electronically to your pharmacy. Your patient picks it up from you. You get the fill. Everyone wins.

Why Partner

What's in it for your pharmacy

More prescription fills

Patients who couldn't get a prescription before now can, and they fill it with you.

Happier patients

You become the pharmacy that actually helped them get the care they needed. That's loyalty.

Zero cost to you

No fees, no contracts, no obligations. We provide the QR materials for free.

Fully compliant

No kickbacks, no referral fees. The patient always chooses their pharmacy. Clean and simple.

Common Questions

FAQ

Is this legal?

Yes. There are no referral fees or kickbacks of any kind. NPCWoods does not pay your pharmacy and your pharmacy does not pay NPCWoods. The patient always has the option to choose any pharmacy they want. This is simply a referral resource for your uninsured patients.

What does my pharmacy need to do?

Display a small QR code card on your counter. That's it. When a patient needs care, they scan it, text Chris, and get treated. If they need a prescription, we send it to you electronically.

What states are covered?

NPCWoods is currently licensed in Arizona, New Mexico, Georgia, and North Carolina. If your pharmacy is in one of these states, we can serve your patients.

What conditions can you treat?

Common acute conditions: UTIs, sinus infections, strep throat, ear infections, cold/flu, allergies, skin rashes, and medication refills. If something is outside our scope, we'll let the patient know and recommend they seek in-person care.

How do patients pay?

Patients pay $59 directly to NPCWoods after their visit. Your pharmacy fills the prescription like any other e-script. The patient pays for their medication at your counter as usual.

Can any patient use this?

Absolutely. We keep it simple, $59 flat fee, no paperwork. Any patient can use NPCWoods. Some patients prefer us because we're faster and cheaper than a typical copay.

Ready to partner?

Text or email Chris to get started. We'll send you everything you need, QR codes, counter cards, and any info for your team.
