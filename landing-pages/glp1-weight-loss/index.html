When Text-Based Care May Not Be Right

Some situations need in-person evaluation. Chris reviews safety factors first and will tell you if text-based care is not the right lane.

In-person care may be safer with:

  • Severe or worsening abdominal pain
  • Persistent vomiting or signs of dehydration
  • Pregnancy, breastfeeding, or actively trying to conceive
  • A history of serious pancreas, thyroid, or gastrointestinal conditions
  • Any symptom that feels urgent or unsafe to manage by text

If you are not sure, text Chris and ask. If the safer answer is in-person care, he will say that clearly.
