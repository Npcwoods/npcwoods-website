Questions

The doubts we should answer before they ask.

What does the $59 visit include?

A text-based visit with Chris Woods, clinical review, clear next steps, and a prescription sent to your pharmacy when medically appropriate.

Is this a chatbot?

No. Chris Woods, MSN, APRN, FNP-C, reviews visits personally. The technology keeps things simple. The clinical judgment is human.

When is text-based care not enough?

Chest pain, severe abdominal pain, neurological symptoms, severe dehydration, serious injuries, or anything that feels like an emergency needs in-person care. NPCWoods should say that plainly.

Where is NPCWoods available?

Arizona, Colorado, Georgia, Idaho, Iowa, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, North Carolina, Oregon, and Utah. You need to be physically located in one of those states during the visit.
