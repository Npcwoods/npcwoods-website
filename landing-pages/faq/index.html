How It Works

NPCWoods is text-based telemedicine. You text (480) 639-4722 describing your symptoms and upload a photo if needed. Chris Woods, a double board-certified Nurse Practitioner, personally reviews your case and sends a prescription to your local pharmacy, all by text message. No video call, no scheduling, no waiting room. Most visits are completed same day, often within a few hours. The entire visit costs $59 flat.

Async (asynchronous) telemedicine means you communicate with your healthcare provider by text message rather than a live video or phone call. You send your symptoms anytime, day or night, and your provider responds when they review your case, usually within hours. There are no appointments to schedule and no waiting rooms. It's faster, cheaper, and more convenient than traditional telehealth for straightforward conditions like UTIs, sinus infections, and strep throat.

Most visits are completed same day, often within a few hours. During business hours, responses are typically faster. Evening and weekend messages may take slightly longer but are still answered the same day in most cases. Prescriptions are sent directly to your pharmacy as soon as Chris completes the review.

Yes. Text-based telemedicine is HIPAA-compliant and evidence-based. For common acute conditions like UTIs, sinus infections, strep throat, and skin infections, a thorough text-based clinical history is sufficient for diagnosis and treatment according to IDSA (Infectious Diseases Society of America) and CDC guidelines. Studies show that symptom-based diagnosis for uncomplicated UTIs is accurate approximately 95% of the time without requiring physical examination or lab testing.
