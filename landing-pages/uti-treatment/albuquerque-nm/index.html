I'd Rather Lose Your $59 Than Miss Something Serious

Pure transparency: not everything that burns is a simple UTI. If any of this sounds like you, skip the text and go get seen:

  • Fever + back pain. A UTI that's moved to your kidneys is a different animal. You might need IV antibiotics. and that means an ER, not a text message.
  • Significant blood in your urine. A little pink can happen with a bad UTI. A lot of blood needs evaluation.
  • Can't keep anything down. If you're vomiting, you can't take oral antibiotics. You need an IV.
  • Pregnant or might be. UTIs during pregnancy need in-person monitoring. Period.
  • Young kids. Children under 2 with urinary symptoms need to be seen face-to-face.

If none of that applies and it's the standard burning-and-can't-stop-peeing situation? That's exactly what I'm here for. Text me.
