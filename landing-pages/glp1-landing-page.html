Questions? Here Are Answers.

What exactly is oral GLP-1? +

It's an FDA-approved once-daily pill that works the same way as injectable GLP-1 medications. It mimics a natural hormone that regulates appetite and blood sugar, you feel full longer and eat less. The key difference: it's a tablet, not an injection.

Is the pill as effective as the injection? +

Clinical trials showed comparable weight loss, 16.6% average body weight lost with full adherence. Real-world data also shows similar outcomes between oral and injectable forms at 6 months. Your provider will help determine which option fits your situation best.

What are the side effects? +

Most common: nausea, especially early on. Some people experience vomiting, diarrhea, or constipation. These are usually mild to moderate and tend to decrease as your body adjusts. I start you on a low dose and increase gradually to minimize this. Serious side effects are rare but we'll discuss them during your consultation.

How do I take it? +

One pill, first thing in the morning, on an empty stomach. Swallow with no more than 4 ounces of plain water. Wait 30 minutes before eating, drinking anything else, or taking other medications. That's it. Most people find it easy to build into their routine.

How much does it all cost? +

$59 for your consultation (one-time). $199/month for the medication (ongoing). These are two separate charges, the consultation covers your evaluation and treatment plan, the medication is your monthly supply shipped to your door. No memberships, no "platform fees," no hidden charges. Cancel anytime.

How quickly will I see results? +

Most patients notice appetite changes within 1-2 weeks. Measurable weight loss typically starts within the first month. The most significant results come over 3-6 months as you reach your full therapeutic dose. I'll monitor your progress and adjust as needed.

What states are you licensed in? +

Arizona, Colorado, Georgia, Idaho, Iowa, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, North Carolina, Oregon, and Utah. If you're outside these states, I can't help directly, but I can point you in the right direction.

What if it doesn't work for me? +

Text me. If you're not seeing results or having issues, we adjust. Different dose, different approach, or honest conversation about whether this is the right path for you. There's no contract locking you in, cancel anytime, no questions asked.
