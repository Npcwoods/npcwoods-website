Go straight to Erlanger Blue Ridge for:

  • Chest pain or pressure, especially with arm or jaw pain
  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath at rest
  • Sudden severe headache unlike anything before
  • Signs of stroke: face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty
  • High fever with stiff neck or altered consciousness
  • Severe abdominal pain, especially lower right (possible appendix)
  • Heavy bleeding that won't stop
