Good people, thin healthcare access. Let's fix that.

Iowa's got a problem that doesn't get enough attention. Small towns across the state have been losing their healthcare providers for years. The family practice shuts down, and suddenly you're driving 30-40 minutes to the next town over just to get someone to look at a sore throat.

And if you're farming? You can't exactly leave the operation for half a day during planting or harvest. Your schedule doesn't care that the only available appointment is Tuesday at 2 PM in a city 45 minutes away.

Here's the deal. I'm Chris Woods, a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner, and I'm licensed in Iowa. You text me what's going on, I ask a few questions, and if you need medication I send the prescription to your nearest Hy-Vee, Walgreens, or whatever pharmacy is in your town. Fifty-nine dollars. No paperwork, no hassle.

I treat UTIs, sinus infections, ear infections, dental pain, sore throats, skin rashes, the bread-and-butter stuff that used to be a quick trip to the family doc. That kind of care shouldn't disappear just because your town is small.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a Iowa Nurse Practitioner prescribe antibiotics online? +

Yes, a licensed Family Nurse Practitioner in Iowa can prescribe antibiotics online. Chris Woods at NPCWoods reviews symptoms via secure text consults and sends appropriate prescriptions same-day to your preferred local pharmacy.

Is text-based telemedicine legal in Iowa? +

Yes, text-based asynchronous telemedicine is fully legal and regulated in Iowa. NPCWoods operates in complete compliance with state medical board guidelines, providing secure, HIPAA-compliant consults without requiring video appointments.

How long does an online urgent care visit take in Iowa? +

A text-based urgent care consult in Iowa takes under five minutes to start. Once submitted, our double board-certified Nurse Practitioner reviews your symptoms and sends your prescription within hours.
